Eclipse Odyssey

Eclipse Odyssey is a VR game developed in Unity using C#. The main character, Asuna, must navigate through Etherium and Celestium, overcoming obstacles to recover the Ethereal and Celestial crystals and save Infinity Nexus. I worked on the Etherium section, which features a maze. Initially, it was designed as a mirror maze to increase difficulty and engagement, but implementing multiple cameras caused significant lag. However, without the mirror maze, players can still be engaged in combatting enemies to retrieve the Etherium crystal

Cosmo Wars

This is a simplified VR aircraft simulation game, inspired by a minigame from Mario Party 8 and the flying from Hogwarts Legacy. I designed it using my understanding of throttle and handle physics, which I learned from watching motorsports. In the game, players attempt to pilot an aircraft while combatting their enemies. They must manoeuvre the handle to control direction and adjust the throttle to regulate speed, all while navigating toward a space station. They can also activate the aircraft's projectile to attack enemy's. For the sake of the video, the gameplay has been shortened and further simplified.

Blow Your Mind

Blow Your Mind is a VR game developed in Unity using C# . The game was inspired by Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes, aiming to indulge players in a problem solving game. The room features navigation portals that allow players to move around and search for key components required to defuse the bomb. Players must explore the environment, identify the correct items, and assemble them into the bomb model before the five minute countdown runs out.

Monster World

Monster World is a game website centered around battles and clans. I worked on mainly the clan system, allowing players to create or join clans, which hopes to build friendships and community. Each clan has 3 rooms: The Hive, Rock, Paper, Scissors, and Disco. These rooms are inspired by my favourite childhood game, Club Penguin.

The Hive is inspired by The Sims, allowing clan members to use their clan points to purchase furnitures and customise their clan rooms. Rewards will be given to the best hive every month.

Chat Messages enables players to communicate with friends and clanmates. Players can send, retrieve, edit, and delete messages, creating interactive conversations.

Rock, Paper, Scissors is a clan game where players can earn clan points by competing against NPC masters in a randomized game of rock, paper, scissors. In future updates, players can have real-time matches against theirs clanmates.

Clan Data is designed for clan leaders and co-leaders to monitor their clanmates' participation. Clan leaders and co-leaders can manage roles and remove inactive members.

Disco Cosmo is inspired by Club Penguin's Night Club, offering a social space for clan members to bond. Currently, players can jump, but future updates will introduce dance competitions, real-time dancing with clan members and live chat.

Create Clan is where players can create their own private or public clans.
